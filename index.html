ABOUT PASQUALE

Biography

Pasquale J. Colavita is the founder and managing partner of the firm, which maintains offices in Philadelphia and Cherry Hill, New Jersey. The son of a long-time local contractor, he understands the needs of small businesses, especially those that are family owned and operated. Pasquale has extensive experience representing clients in the food and beverage industry, including restaurants, bars, and wineries, as well as construction companies.

A second-generation immigrant, who spoke only Italian until he was five, Pasquale is proud to be the first attorney in his family. When he's not practicing law, he enjoys spending time with his wife and teenage son and daughter.
